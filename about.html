My passion

I love helping people in general! I usually do not specially carve out time to volunteer, but I help the people around me whenever I can 😊. Be it being a listening ear or helping individuals find their way around the neighbourhood, I enjoy helping anyone within my abilities❤️!

My hobbies

In my free time, I mainly paint using poster colours 🎨. It is really a time-consuming past-time as hours must be set aside for me to enjoy the process of mixing colours and applying them on canvas. (Maybe I am just not skillful enough to complete an aesthetic piece in a short time 😢.)

Recently, I am in the midst of discovering colouring with crayons 🖍️. (I will upload the pieces soon once I am done 😊.)

I also love to dance free-style in my own room! The ability to move my body freely is just so liberating and it has given me the sense of hope that I can control my body and mind 💃.

My education journey

Recently, I have graduated from Eunoia Junior College (EJC). These 2 years (2021 - 2022) have been really challenging but it was fruitful to go through the "Tough times don't last, tough people do" era 💪.

I was also a member of Eunoia Drama. I am really grateful for my instructor, teachers-in-charge and peers for always supporting me and nurture me to become a more confident individual 🥰. Shoutout to my batchmates in Drama to making this tertiary education journey much more bearable as we shared all the good and bad times!


From 2017 to 2020, I have spent my developing years in CHIJ St Nicholas Girls' School . I really enjoyed building relationships with my teachers and peers, as well as constantly improving myself as a person.

As a member of St John Singapore , I was fortunate enough to represent Singapore with my school team in the 32nd Malaysia-Singapore First-Aid and Nursing Competition in the Nursing Cadet Category in 2019.

The sisterly love culture is so heartwarming, where we call one another "姐姐" (elder sister) and "妹妹" (younger sister). What I really miss is the morning and afternoon prayers in school which kept my mind at ease ❤️.
