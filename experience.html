Tutoring

Since August 2023, I have been tutoring primary school and secondary school students. At first, it simply saw the opportunity as a way to make an additional source of income. Along the way, I have interacted with students from all walks of life. It is always heartwarming to see that they are putting in the effort to improve themselves.

Interacting with younger children helps me reflect. As people around them, we can have a significant impact on these younger beings, whether we are conscious of it or not. Every encouragement can serve as a motivation for them to work harder, and play an important role to fulfill their potential. It is crucial to be careful with how we make them feel through our words and actions as it can shape their perceptions, which ultimately shape their beliefs and values.

It is meaningful to empower these children to realise what they are capable of and achieve what they can. By helping these children in their academics as well as instilling hope and confidence in them at the same time makes this journey extremely fulfilling! ü•∞

Leadership

A leadership opportunity that shaped me into an individual who can better understand others' situations is being a Project Lead for Junior College 2 Ora House-based Values-in-Action (VIA) Activity on Deep Dive Day 2 2022 in Eunoia Junior College (EJC).

From crafting the initial proposal to collating ideas and feedback from our beneficiary, the Non-Teaching Staff (NTS) in EJC, and finally executing the individualised activities for each class, it was a fruitful journey for me. It was not easy to negotiate and not miss out the different stakeholders' intentions and resources when communicating with them. Through this journey of leading, the importance of mindful listening was the most significant lesson learnt.

Besides, there were also some unexpected problems that surfaced though, such me falling sick for 2 weeks. This pushed back the execution of tasks that are supposed to be carried out in these 2 weeks. Thankfully, tasks were planned ahead so we were not behind time by a lot. From this snippet, I have learnt that ahead-of-time planning is necessary, but adaptability will also be an essential asset for a leader.
