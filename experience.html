Leadership

The recent leadership opportunity that shaped me into an individual who can better understand others' situations is being a Project Lead for Junior College 2 Ora House-based Values-in-Action (VIA) Activity on Deep Dive Day 2 2022 in Eunoia Junior College (EJC).

From crafting the initial proposal to collating ideas and feedback from our beneficiary, the Non-Teaching Staff (NTS) in EJC, and finally executing the individualised activities for each class, it was a fruitful journey for me. It was not easy to negotiate and not miss out the different stakeholders' intentions and resources when communicating with them. Through this journey of leading, the importance of mindful listening was the most significant lesson learnt.

Besides, there were also some unexpected problems that surfaced though, such me falling sick for 2 weeks. This pushed back the execution of tasks that are supposed to be carried out in these 2 weeks. Thankfully, tasks were planned ahead so we were not behind time by a lot. From this snippet, I have learnt that ahead-of-time planning is necessary, but adaptability will also be an essential asset for a leader.
